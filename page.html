MiMosWeb is designed to reduce the work burden of housekeeping staff who is required to visit each floor for monitoring the milk containers and refill it when its near empty. This is a challenging task especially in facility like TCS Kochi, having two blocks with 13 floors each.

MiMosWeb provides solution to monitor the milk containers and notify the housekeeping staff when a refill is required, thus minimising the manual efforts required and improving the overall efficiency.

How to stop notifications from MiMosApp?
Follow the steps:
settings->notifications then turn off the notification button.
How does Mimosapp work?
Mimosapp notify & let you know the levels of milk in the coffee vending machine by collecting data from sensors and analyse the data.
What to do if the application is not working properly?
1:Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
2:Make your phone's android version in compactible.
3:Proper working of the sensors in the vending machine.
4:Make sure the sensors are properly placed(facing to the inside of the container).
What happens after refilling the container?
Further notifications about the refilled machine will stop.
